ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

The Crappy Page: Berkshirehathaway

Looks to be just a little company page with some links added to it.

  • Contrast: This websites colors are very bland but they dont clash and the contrast is fine
  • Repition: The links are all spread out accross the page and basically is the main attraction.
  • Alignment: Though the website is poor, everything has its place and is well aligned.
  • Proximity: In my opinion the links are too spaced out, the header and footer text is too smushed together, and the overall page has an add proximity

The Better Webpage: Nike

This the online shop for the sports/clothing Brand Nike

  • Crontrast: Coloring is straight and to the point, it has a well layed out color scheme that is easy no the eyes.
  • Repition: It is hard to claim that a clothing website has Repition since it is repeating the same graphic for different items over and over again , thats just how it is
  • Alignment: All of the images and links are well aligned and the website has a user friendly lay out where things are easy to find
  • Proximity: The text has good spacing, the images are well set within the boxes for each item, the page flows well. Again it is a user friendly well layed out site
