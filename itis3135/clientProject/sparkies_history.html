Our History

My Great grand pops Clayton Bud Sparks business as standard oil was called city service in spindale. When sparkies opened it was in Forest City, originally a Chrysler dealership which became Cadillac, oldsmobile then subaru…

When Bud returned from serving in WW2 he worked in the local textile mill in Spindale, NC. He saved his money and bought a Standard Oil franchise in Spindale leaving the textile mill behind. He along with his brother then bought a piece of land nearby & began selling used cars, traveling to Detroit and buying as many cars as they could to supply the local market. Selling cars was such a success he sold the Standard Oil business to focus on selling cars full time. This used car business evolved into the purchase of a Cadillac & Oldsmobile Dealership in Spindale primarily to gain access to financing. Sparkies Motors operated throughout Bud’s lifetime passing on to his sons & eventually closing its doors after more than 50 years of operation with the retirement of his eldest son and the passing of his youngest.

My Father and I are proud to continue the legacy of my Great Grandfather, and are excited to see how things turn out and where this new business will take us!
